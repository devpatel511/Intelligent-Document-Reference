Glossary of Terms
=================
RAG – Retrieval-Augmented Generation
LLM – Large Language Model
OCR – Optical Character Recognition
Embedding – A dense vector representation of text
Chunking – Splitting documents into smaller segments
BM25 – A probabilistic ranking function used in information retrieval
HNSW – Hierarchical Navigable Small World (approximate nearest neighbor index)
Cosine Similarity – A measure of similarity between two non-zero vectors
TF-IDF – Term Frequency–Inverse Document Frequency
FAISS – Facebook AI Similarity Search library
