Dataset 3: Large PDFs
=====================
This dataset contains large (50+ page) public domain PDF documents
for testing the PDF ingestion and chunking pipeline.

Contents:
- us_constitution.pdf – US Constitution (Government Printing Office)
- nist_cybersecurity_framework.pdf – NIST Cybersecurity Framework v1.1
- un_declaration_human_rights.pdf – Universal Declaration of Human Rights
- deep_learning_nature.pdf – Deep Learning review paper (Nature 2015)
